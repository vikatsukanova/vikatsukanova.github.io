About

Where: Vancouver, BC, Canada
When: 8-9 June, 2016

Mission Blue is a annual art festival devoted to ocean pollution awareness that takes place on the first weekend of June in Vancouver. Audience is exposed to different kind of art, ranging from sculpture to interactive installations. Also the festival features several guest speakers to expand on the topic.
