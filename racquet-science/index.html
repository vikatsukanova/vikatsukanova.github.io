It's not Rocket science,
it's Racquet Science!

Centrally located and neatly tucked away in one of Toronto's well-established neighbourhoods, the Racquet Science is the vibrant home of Toronto's outdoor tennis enthusiasts and the hub around which our entire neighbourhood has grown up.

Enjoy daily use of our 7 outdoor hard surface courts (including 3 lit courts for night play) with our convenient online court booking system.

Book Now

We have programs for young and old, including lessons, tennis camps, ladders, weekly leagues and drop-in games, monthly tournaments and regular social events.

Nets go up in early Spring and stay up until the snow flies in late-Fall. Our rates are low, and there are no additional court fees. Membership and lessons information is available here.
