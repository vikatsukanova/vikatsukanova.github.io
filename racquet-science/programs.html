Membership

Racquet Science offers different membership options. For unlimited access to the facility and participation in tournaments. Our membership options are based on how much tennis you want to play. Your choices include up to 120 bookings.

Membership privileges include:

  • - Six day advanced booking
  • - Once you become a member there are no additional court fees!
  • - On-line "Looking For a Game Function"
  • - Group and Private Lesson Programs (additional costs apply)
  • - On-site Stringing and Racquet Science Shop Services
